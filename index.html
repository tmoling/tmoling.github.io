Now I'm looking for a new challenge. Past jobs have shown me how much I enjoy technology, collaboration and getting the details right. I recently finished Northwestern University's web development program where I upped my coding game and freshened up some skills in team leadership and project management.

In my free time you'll find me trying out new restaurants, going for a run, watching NBA basketball (Go Grizzlies!) or refining my recipe for the perfect peanut butter and honey sandwich.
